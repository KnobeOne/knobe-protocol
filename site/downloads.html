All materials are released under CC BY 4.0. The normative protocol definition is in the Spec. Nine conformance test vectors are provided for canonical compatibility testing; additional sealed examples and adversarial hardening files are used for browser Lens validation. See test-vectors/.

KNOBE is an open protocol for auditable plain-text knowledge objects that carry their source, history, limits, and obligations across human and AI systems. Built for accessibility, interoperability, and portability.
